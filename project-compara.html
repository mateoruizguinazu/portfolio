Redefining Trust Through UX & SEO

A case study on redesigning the ComparaOnline homepage to boost organic acquisition and empower users to make informed financial decisions.

Overview The Challenge The Solution Results

Project Overview

Upon joining ComparaOnline, I focused on enhancing customer trust and driving organic acquisition through strategic design. My primary objective was to empower users by creating a transparent, user-friendly experience, positioning ComparaOnline as the go-to platform for financial product comparisons in Latin America. Collaborating with the Conversion and Acquisition teams, I developed a design strategy that balanced user needs, business goals, and SEO performance.

My Role

Product Designer

Timeline

11 months (Jan - Nov 2021)

Focus

UX Design, SEO Strategy, Design Systems

The Challenge

The core challenge was twofold. From a user's perspective, the platform felt complex and transactional, creating a barrier of trust and making it difficult to make confident financial decisions. From a business perspective, the site struggled with poor search engine rankings, leading to a heavy reliance on paid marketing and low organic customer acquisition. My task was to solve both problems simultaneously: redesign the experience to be more human and educational, while also building a strong foundation for SEO to drive sustainable growth.

The Solution

The solution was a complete redesign of the customer acquisition funnel and the creation of a scalable design system. I designed a humanized, educational onboarding experience that guided users through their options, building trust and clarity. To support this and future initiatives, I engineered and migrated the company to a new Figma token-based design system. This system streamlined workflows and created a consistent, high-quality user experience across the platform.
